Sentiment Analysis

What is Sentiment Analysis ?

Sentiment Analysis is the process of determining whether a piece of writing is positive, negative or neutral. It’s also known as opinion mining, deriving the opinion or attitude of a speaker.Say you want to know if people on Twitter think that Chinese food in San Francisco is good or bad. Twitter sentiment analysis will answer this question. You can even learn why people think the food is good or bad, by extracting the exact words that indicate why people did or didn't like the food. If too salty shows as a common theme, for example, you immediately have a better idea of why consumers aren’t happy.

A common use case for this technology is to discover how people feel about a particular topic.

  • We can determine what people feel about a particular thing
  • Helpful in determining their likes and opinions about certain specific things
  • Data from Sentiment Analysis can be applied to a wide range of fields in commercial as well as personal purposes
